Sliders

Description

This sliders recipe is simple, easy to make, and a great way to create a delicious meal for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Ground Beef
  • 1/2 White Onion, diced
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 12 hawaiian rolls or slider buns
  • Cheese slices of your choice

Steps

  1. Prehead the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Combine ground beef, white onion, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a large glass baking dish, 9x13 in recommended
  3. Press into an even layer in the baking dish and pop into the oven for 20 minutes
  4. Drain excess fat
  5. Take out the meat and set aside, put the bottom buns in the dish, set the meat on top, then the cheese and top buns
  6. Pop in the oven for 20 more minutes
  7. Enjoy!
