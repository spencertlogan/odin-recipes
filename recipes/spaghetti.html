Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is simple, easy to make, and a great way to create a delicious meal in just 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • 1 jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • Spaghetti pasta or Angel hair pasta
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Brown Ground Beef in a Saucepan over medium heat
  2. Drain excess fat and add Spaghetti Sauce
  3. Season with Garlic Powder and Italian Seasoning to taste
  4. Follow directions on noodle package to cook noodles al dente
  5. Plate noodles and Meat sauce, top with Parmesan cheese, and enjoy!
