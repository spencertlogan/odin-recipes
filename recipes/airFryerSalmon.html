Air Fryer Salmon

Description

This air fryer salmon recipe is simple, easy to make, and a great way to create a delicious meal in just 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Salmon
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat Air Fryer to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Lay Salmon skin down on aluminum foil
  3. Coat Salmon in Olive Oil
  4. Add Salt, Black Pepper, and Garlic Powder
  5. Cook for 10 minutes
