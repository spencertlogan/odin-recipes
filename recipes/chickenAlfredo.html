Chicken Alfredo

Description

This Chicken Alfredo recipe is simple, easy to make, and a great way to create a delicious meal in just 25 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Chicken Breast
  • 1 jar Alfredo Sauce
  • Pasta of choice, fettucine or penne recommended
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken Breasts over medium heat until tender
  2. Season with Salt, Pepper, and Garlic powder
  3. Slice chicken into bite-size chunks
  4. Cook pasta according to package directions and shoot for al dente
  5. Drain water and add Alfredo sauce to noodles
  6. Season Pasta and Alfredo sauce to taste
  7. Plate noodles and Chicken and enjoy!
